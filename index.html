About Us

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complemented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store.

There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.

Please note: we are a cashless venue. The range closes at 10pm with last balls at 9pm.

TOPTRACER RANGE

Track every shot with smart range technology, enjoy interactive games, and make practice feel more exciting.

It is great for casual players, first-timers, and golfers working on distance and accuracy.

MR MULLIGAN'S ADVENTURE GOLF

Explore a fun themed course designed for families, friends, and relaxed group outings.

The playful obstacles and lively setting make it easy to enjoy whether you are competing or just having fun.

GOLF LESSONS

Build confidence with coaching tailored to beginners, juniors, and players improving their technique.

Each lesson focuses on practical progress, stronger fundamentals, and enjoying the game more.
